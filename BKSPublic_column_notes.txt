BKSPublic.parquet - Column Modification Notes
==============================================
15,511 rows x 376 columns

This is a ~1.6% stratified subsample of the full BKS dataset (~980K responses).
Reference the GT source file for original question wording.

These notes describe columns that were binned, combined, renamed, or computed
in ways that differ from the original GT survey questions. If you're looking
for a GT question and can't find it, check below â€” it may have been merged,
renamed, or dropped.


BINNED / COLLAPSED COLUMNS
---------------------------

politics
  Original: 5 levels (Very Liberal, Liberal, Moderate, Conservative, Very Conservative)
  Now: 3 levels (Liberal, Moderate, Conservative)
  Very Liberal + Liberal -> Liberal; Very Conservative + Conservative -> Conservative

bmi
  Original: 10 bins from height + weight
  Now: 2 levels (Not overweight, Overweight+)
  Underweight + Normal -> "Not overweight"; Overweight + Obese -> "Overweight+"

straightness
  Original: Computed from attractedtomasculine + attractedtofeminine (continuous).
  Now: 5 levels (Gay, Leaning gay, Bi, Leaning straight, Straight)
  Source columns (attractedtomasculine, attractedtofeminine, penis/vagina
  attraction) have been removed.

sexcount ("How many people have you had sex with?")
  Original: Free-text numeric entry (0-600)
  Now: 5 bins (0, 1-2, 3-7, 8-20, 21+)

How "sexually liberated" was your upbringing? (fs700v2)
  Original: 7 levels (Highly repressed through Highly liberated)
  Now: 3 levels (Repressed, Neutral, Liberated)
  Highly/Moderately/Slightly repressed -> Repressed
  Equally repressed/liberated -> Neutral
  Slightly/Moderately/Highly liberated -> Liberated


COMBINED / MERGED COLUMNS
--------------------------

childhood_adversity
  Combined from two separate GT questions:
    - "Were you (at least semiregularly) abused as a child?" (abuse)
    - "As a child, were you the victim of sexual assault?" (sexualAssaultLevel)
  Now: 2 levels (None, Any)
  "Any" = answered Yes to either or both. Original columns removed.

childhood_gender_tolerance
  Combined from two separate GT questions:
    - "In your childhood culture, how did people feel about traditional
       gender role violations?" (tkpfq6j)
    - "In your childhood culture, how did people feel about visible
       genderbending?" (gjt7igv)
  Both were 6-level scales from "So normal nobody noticed" to "Nearly
  unheard of, if done generated full community eviction."
  Averaged the two (mapped to 0-5 numeric), then binned:
    Low avg (intolerant cultures) -> "Intolerant"
    Mid avg -> "Medium"
    High avg (tolerant cultures) -> "Tolerant"
  Original columns removed.

TotalMentalIllness
  Original: Count of mental illnesses checked (0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5+)
  Now: 2 levels (None, Any)
  0 -> None; 1+ -> Any

Personally, your preferred relationship style is: (4jib23m)
  Original: 3 levels (Mono, Middle, Poly)
  Now: 2 levels (Monogamous, Not monogamous)
  Middle + Poly -> "Not monogamous"


COMPUTED COLUMNS (not directly from GT questions)
-------------------------------------------------

These are averages/sums of multiple GT sub-questions. They don't correspond
to a single GT question:

  opennessvariable          OCEAN personality (avg of sub-questions, range -6 to 6)
  consciensiousnessvariable OCEAN personality (avg of sub-questions, range -6 to 6)
  extroversionvariable      OCEAN personality (avg of sub-questions, range -6 to 6)
  neuroticismvariable       OCEAN personality (avg of sub-questions, range -6 to 6)
  agreeablenessvariable     OCEAN personality (avg of sub-questions, range -6 to 6)
  powerlessnessvariable     Computed score (range -9 to 9)
  totalfetishcategory       Count of fetish categories with any interest
  bondageaverage            Average across bondage sub-items
  Total* columns            Sum/count columns for various fetish categories
                            (e.g., Totalbondage, Totalnonconsent1, etc.)


DROPPED GT QUESTIONS (not in this dataset)
------------------------------------------

Demographics removed:
  - country
  - ethnicity/race
  - religion_importance (how important is religion to you)
  - sexage (age of first intercourse)
  - "Growing up, your family was..." (social class)
  - "Which category fits you best?" (detailed gender identity beyond cis/trans)
  - Relationship status 
  - Sex work questions

Source columns removed (replaced by computed versions):
  - attractedtomasculine, attractedtofeminine (replaced by straightness)
  - penis/vagina attraction columns (replaced by straightness)
  - abuse, sexualAssaultLevel (replaced by childhood_adversity)
  - Gender role violations + genderbending (replaced by childhood_gender_tolerance)

Content removed:
  - All pedophilia-related questions (gate + sub-questions)
  - Abuse sub-questions (verbal/physical by man/woman, neglect)
  - All *smost columns (sparse "second most erotic" picks)

Duplicate GT columns removed:
  - Several columns had GT bug duplicates (same question, two column IDs).
    The shorter-fill version was dropped in each case.
