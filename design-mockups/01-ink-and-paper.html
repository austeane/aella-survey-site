01 Global Caveats
Gated Missingness
Some questions were only shown after prerequisite answers. Missing values may reflect skip-logic, not non-response.
Filter or impute with care when analyzing columns with structural NAs.
Late-Added Questions
Certain variables were added partway through the survey's collection period, creating cohort-dependent missingness.
Consider temporal bias when interpreting null rates above 30%.
